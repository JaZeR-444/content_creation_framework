# 01_Strategy_and_Planning

This phase establishes the foundational elements of your content strategy. Before creating any content, you must have clarity on your audience, positioning, goals, and overall direction.

## Overview

Strategy and Planning contains 10 essential modules that guide you through building a rock-solid content foundation:

1. **Positioning Statement** - Define your unique value proposition
2. **90-Day Plan** - Set actionable, measurable short-term goals
3. **Ideal Viewer Profile** - Understand your target audience deeply
4. **Content Pillars** - Establish core topics for your content
5. **Idea Backlog** - Build a prioritized list of content ideas
6. **Publishing Cadence** - Create a sustainable release schedule
7. **Competitor Scan** - Identify gaps and opportunities
8. **Brand One-Pager** - Codify your brand identity and voice
9. **6-Week Content Calendar** - Plan your content pipeline
10. **Seed Keyword List** - Create your SEO foundation

## How to Use This Phase

1. **Start with the Master File**: Review `Strategy-and-Planning-Master-File.md` for a comprehensive view of all modules
2. **Work Sequentially**: Complete modules in order (001 through 010) for best results
3. **Use Input Forms**: Each module has a corresponding input form in the `User-Input-Forms/` folder
4. **Take Your Time**: This phase sets the direction for everything that follows - don't rush it

## Files in This Folder

- **Individual Prompt Files** (001-010): Detailed prompts for each strategic module
- **Master File** (.md and .txt): Combined reference document with all 10 modules
- **User-Input-Forms/**: Structured templates for gathering your inputs

## Success Criteria

You've completed this phase when you have:
- A clear positioning statement that differentiates your brand
- Defined content pillars aligned with audience interests
- A 6-week content calendar ready to execute
- Understanding of your competitive landscape
- A sustainable publishing cadence

## Next Steps

After completing Strategy and Planning, move to **02_Content_Creation_and_Production** to bring your strategic vision to life.
