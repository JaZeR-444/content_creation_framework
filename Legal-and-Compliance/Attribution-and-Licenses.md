# Attribution and Licenses

## Purpose

This document tracks all third-party assets used in your content, including music, stock photos, videos, fonts, graphics, and any other licensed materials. Proper attribution protects you legally and respects creators' rights.

## How to Use This Document

1. **Before using any asset**, verify license and add to tracking
2. **Document immediately**, don't wait until later
3. **Update per content piece**, link to Content-IDs
4. **Keep license files** saved in appropriate asset folders
5. **Review regularly** for expired licenses or missing info
