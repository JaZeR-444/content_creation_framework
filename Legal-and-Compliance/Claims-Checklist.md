# Claims Checklist

## Purpose

This checklist helps you verify claims, avoid legal issues, and ensure content accuracy before publishing. Use this for every piece of content that makes factual claims, reviews products, or discusses legal/medical/financial topics.

## How to Use This Checklist

1. **Review before scripting** - Consider claims during content planning
2. **Complete before publishing** - Don't skip this step
3. **Keep documentation** - Save sources and research
4. **Update as needed** - Add items relevant to your niche
5. **Consult professionals** - When in doubt, get expert advice

## Red Flags - Do Not Publish If:

- [ ] Unable to verify a major claim
- [ ] Missing required disclosures
- [ ] Potential defamation or libel
- [ ] Using copyrighted content without license
- [ ] Making medical claims without disclaimer
- [ ] Guaranteeing financial returns
- [ ] Missing consent from identifiable people
- [ ] Violates platform policies
- [ ] High legal risk without attorney review

**If any red flags are checked, resolve before publishing.**
