# Content Creation Framework Overview

This document provides a high-level overview of the combined content within each major section of the Content Creation Framework. Each section's content has been consolidated into a `master_content.txt` file for easy access and review.

## 01_Strategy_and_Planning

This file contains comprehensive prompts and guidelines for establishing the foundational elements of your content strategy. It covers:

*   **Positioning Statement:** Crafting a clear and concise statement that defines your brand, audience, problem, and promise.
*   **90-Day Plan:** Developing actionable, measurable goals and strategies for short-term growth.
*   **Ideal Viewer Profile:** Deeply understanding your target audience through detailed profiles.
*   **Content Pillars:** Defining core topics that align with your audience's interests and your channel's focus.
*   **Idea Backlog:** Building a prioritized list of content ideas tailored to your resources.
*   **Publishing Cadence:** Establishing a realistic and sustainable schedule for content release.
*   **Competitor Scan:** Analyzing competitors to identify content gaps and unique positioning opportunities.
*   **Brand One-Pager:** Codifying your channel's brand identity, voice, tone, and visual style.
*   **6-Week Content Calendar:** Generating a detailed content schedule with working titles and supporting details.
*   **Seed Keyword List:** Creating a focused list of keywords and questions for SEO and content planning.

## 02_Content_Creation_and_Production

This file guides you through the practical aspects of content creation and production, ensuring consistency and quality. It includes:

*   **Script Template:** A reusable template for structuring video content with clear hooks, beats, and CTAs.
*   **Pre-Production Checklist:** A repeatable checklist covering outlines, shot lists, props, locations, and B-roll planning.
*   **Standardize Gear Setup:** Guidelines for consistent mic checks, lighting, and camera profiles.
*   **Define a Recording Ritual:** A repeatable ritual for on-set recording, including slate, room tone, and battery management.
*   **Set Folder Structure and Naming:** A clean, scalable folder structure and naming standard for all project files.
*   **Document an Editing Workflow:** A repeatable workflow covering import, rough cut, tightening, captions, color, and audio.
*   **Save Presets:** Standardization of lower thirds, transitions, LUTs, compression, and loudness targets.
*   **Build Thumbnail Template Set:** Reusable thumbnail templates with layout variants for different platforms.
*   **End Screen and Card Template:** Reusable templates for end screens and info cards to increase watch time and conversions.
*   **Write a Backup Plan (3-2-1):** A clear, tested backup and recovery plan adhering to the 3-2-1 rule.

## 03_Optimization_and_Distribution

This file focuses on strategies to optimize your content for discoverability and effectively distribute it across various platforms. It covers:

*   **Keyword Selection per Video:** A process for selecting primary and supporting keywords for each video.
*   **Title Sprint:** Generating and scoring multiple title options for optimal click-through rates.
*   **Description Template:** A ready-to-paste template for video descriptions, including UTM links and chapters.
*   **Maintain a Tag List and Default Upload Settings:** Managing tags and default upload settings for consistency across platforms.
*   **Chapters With Action-Oriented Labels:** Creating effective chapter markers for improved navigation and search.
*   **Playlist Assignment:** Assigning videos to relevant playlists or creating new ones to optimize session watch time.
*   **Follow a Thumbnail Checklist:** A repeatable checklist for creating high-performing thumbnails.
*   **Placement End Screens and Cards:** Strategically placing end screens and info cards to drive engagement.
*   **Distribution Plan Per Video:** A per-video plan for community posts, emails, shorts, and social posts.
*   **Set a Repurpose Workflow:** A workflow for transforming flagship videos into derivative assets like short clips, carousels, and quote images.

## 04_Analysis_and_Iteration

This file provides tools and processes for analyzing content performance, identifying areas for improvement, and iterating on your strategy. It includes:

*   **Build a Dashboard:** Creating a compact performance dashboard for core video KPIs.
*   **Review Retention Graphs and Drop Fix Log:** Analyzing audience retention to identify and fix drop points.
*   **A/B Testing Log:** Maintaining a clean, auditable log for A/B tests on titles, thumbnails, and hooks.
*   **Mine Comments Weekly:** A process for tagging and routing insights from comments (questions, objections, wins, new ideas).
*   **Track a Competitor Watchlist:** A program for tracking competitors, identifying new formats and topics, and generating testable ideas.
*   **Set an Experiment Cadence:** Establishing a repeatable cadence for intentional changes with clear hypotheses.
*   **Prune or Refresh Underperformers:** A decision framework for identifying and acting on underperforming videos.
*   **Refresh Winners:** A process for refreshing high-performing videos with new titles, thumbnails, chapters, and pinned comments.
*   **Schedule Reviews:** A repeatable review cadence for weekly retrospectives, monthly metrics deep dives, and quarterly strategy resets.
*   **Maintain Community Health Tasks:** A system for timely replies, polls, and periodic channel updates to foster community engagement.
