## Quick Script Writing Tips

1. **Write like you talk** - Use conversational language
2. **Short sentences** - Easy to read and deliver
3. **Active voice** - More engaging than passive
4. **Contractions** - "Don't" not "do not"
5. **Pause points** - Mark where to breathe: [PAUSE]
6. **Emphasis** - **Bold** or CAPS for emphasis
7. **Inflection** - (excited), (serious), (questioning)
8. **Time stamps** - Plan your pacing
9. **Flexibility** - Script is a guide, not a prison
10. **Read aloud** - If it's hard to say, rewrite it
