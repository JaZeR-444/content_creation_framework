## Production (Filming)

### Format & Technical

- [ ] **Vertical format** - 9:16 aspect ratio (1080x1920)
- [ ] **Good lighting** - Face visible, well-lit
- [ ] **Clear audio** - No background noise or music too loud
- [ ] **Stable footage** - No shaky camera (use tripod/stabilizer)
- [ ] **High quality** - 1080p minimum resolution
- [ ] **Length appropriate** - Target: 15-60 seconds

### Content Elements

- [ ] **Strong hook** - First 3 seconds grab attention
- [ ] **Fast pacing** - Quick cuts, no dead air
- [ ] **Visual interest** - Movement, color, changes
- [ ] **Text overlays** - Key points on screen (if applicable)
- [ ] **Face on camera** - Personal connection (when appropriate)
- [ ] **Sound/music** - Trending audio or original sound

### Engagement Tactics

- [ ] **Pattern interrupt** - Something unexpected
- [ ] **Loop potential** - Could viewer watch again?
- [ ] **Emotional trigger** - Surprise, humor, curiosity, inspiration
- [ ] **Relatable moment** - "That's so me" feeling
- [ ] **Clear payoff** - Delivered on the promise

## Post-Production (Editing)

### Editing Checklist

- [ ] **Tight cuts** - No wasted seconds
- [ ] **Captions added** - Auto-captions or manual
- [ ] **Text readable** - Large, contrasting, on screen long enough
- [ ] **Color graded** - Consistent, appealing look
- [ ] **Audio balanced** - Music not overpowering voice
- [ ] **Transitions smooth** - Professional feel
- [ ] **Branding subtle** - Logo/watermark if using (not required)

### Content Quality

- [ ] **Hook delivers** - First 3 seconds work
- [ ] **Pacing tight** - No slow moments
- [ ] **Message clear** - Viewer understands the point
- [ ] **Ending strong** - Clear conclusion or CTA
- [ ] **Rewatchable** - Worth watching multiple times

## Short-Form Best Practices

### Hook Formulas (First 3 seconds)

- "Wait for it..."
- "POV: You just..."
- "Here's why..."
- "This is how..."
- "The secret to..."
- Bold statement or controversial opinion
- Unexpected visual or action
- Direct question to viewer

### Content Formulas That Work

- Before/After
- Tutorial/How-to
- Trend participation
- Behind-the-scenes
- Day in the life
- Myths vs. Reality
- Life hack
- Reaction video
- Storytime
- Educational nugget

### Don'ts

- Don't waste the first 3 seconds
- Don't use low-quality footage
- Don't make text too small
- Don't overuse trending sounds (be authentic)
- Don't ignore the comments
- Don't post and disappear
- Don't prioritize quantity over quality
